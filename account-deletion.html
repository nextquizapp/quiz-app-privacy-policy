How to Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to permanently delete your CA Quiz App account and all associated data.

Method 1: Delete Account In-App (Recommended)

Follow these steps to delete your account:

1
Open CA Quiz App

Launch the CA Quiz App on your mobile device and ensure you are logged in to the account you wish to delete.

2
Navigate to Settings

Tap on the Settings tab in the bottom navigation bar (gear icon on the right).

3
Find Account Section

Scroll down to the Account section in the Settings menu.

4
Tap "Delete Account"

Tap on "Delete Account" (shown in red with a warning icon).

5
Review Information

Read the information about what will be deleted and the deletion timeline.

6
Enter Your Password

For security purposes, enter your account password to confirm your identity.

7
Confirm Deletion

Tap "Delete My Account" and confirm in the final warning dialog.

8
Account Deleted

Your account will be immediately deactivated and you will be logged out. Data deletion will begin immediately.

  • Right to Erasure: Delete your account and data (this page)
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing activities
