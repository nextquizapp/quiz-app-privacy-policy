Privacy Policy

CA Quiz App - Chartered Accountancy Exam Preparation

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CA Quiz App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application designed for Chartered Accountancy (CA) exam preparation.

By using CA Quiz App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and optional profile information.
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials managed securely through Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile Preferences: Your exam level (CPT, Foundation, Intermediate, Final), study preferences, and notification settings.

2.2 Quiz and Study Data

  • Quiz Attempts: Your answers to multiple-choice questions (MCQ), descriptive questions, and accounting questions.
  • Performance Data: Scores, marks obtained, time taken, question-wise performance, and progress tracking.
  • Study Notes: Notes and rough work created using the in-app scratchpad feature.
  • Calculator History: Calculations performed using the in-app calculator (stored locally only).
  • Accounting Workings: Your structured answers for accounting questions including journal entries, ledgers, final accounts, etc.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent on questions, navigation patterns, and session duration.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information to improve app stability.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated statistics about app usage and feature popularity.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not access your contacts or phone book
  • We do not collect payment information (app is free)
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • Provide access to quiz questions and study materials
  • Track your progress across different exam topics and papers
  • Save your quiz attempts and allow you to review past performance
  • Enable features like calculator, scratchpad, and note-taking
  • Synchronize your data across devices

3.2 Answer Evaluation

  • MCQ Questions: Automatically scored against correct answers
  • Accounting Questions: Auto-scored using our deterministic scoring engine with tolerance-based numerical comparison
  • Descriptive Questions: Evaluated using AI language models (OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude, or Google Gemini) with rubric-based assessment
Note on AI Evaluation: When your descriptive answers are evaluated by AI, we send only the answer text and rubric criteria to the AI service. Your personal information (name, email) is NOT shared with AI providers. The evaluation is anonymized.

3.3 Performance Analytics and Improvement

  • Generate performance reports and analytics
  • Identify weak areas and suggest topics for revision
  • Improve question quality and difficulty calibration
  • Enhance app features based on usage patterns
  • Fix bugs and improve app stability

3.4 Communication

  • Send important notifications about your quiz attempts and results
  • Notify you about new questions, features, or app updates
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Send exam reminders and study tips (if enabled)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure account management and login credentials
  • Cloud Firestore: Quiz data, user progress, and study history stored in Google's secure cloud infrastructure
  • Local Device Storage: Temporary data, offline access, calculator history, and scratchpad notes

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Firebase security rules to restrict unauthorized access
  • Secure authentication with password hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process information:

5.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

  • Purpose: Authentication, database, analytics, and crash reporting
  • Data Shared: Email, user ID, usage data, device information
  • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 AI Language Models (for Descriptive Answer Evaluation)

  • Services Used: OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude, or Google Gemini
  • Purpose: Evaluate descriptive answers using AI-based rubric assessment
  • Data Shared: Answer text, question text, rubric criteria (anonymized, no personal information)
  • Data Retention: AI providers may retain data for service improvement per their policies
  • Privacy Policies:
    • OpenAI: openai.com/privacy
    • Anthropic: anthropic.com/privacy
    • Google: policies.google.com/privacy

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6.2 When We May Share Information

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (Firebase, AI providers) that help us operate the app, under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

6.3 Aggregated Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized data (e.g., "80% of users found Paper 1 questions helpful") for research or marketing purposes. This data cannot identify individual users.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access your profile, quiz history, and performance data within the app
  • Update Information: Edit your profile, email, and preferences in app settings
  • Download Data: Export your quiz data, notes, and performance reports (available in app settings)

7.2 Communication Preferences

To delete your account and data:

Upon deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days. Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes as required by law.

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")

  • Right to data portability

8. Children's Privacy

CA Quiz App is intended for students preparing for Chartered Accountancy exams, typically aged 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.
